David Nuñez is a designer-turned-front-end-developer. He recently made the transition to this painful yet wonderful new world. What he lacks in experience he makes up for with enthusiasm!

In 2009 he graduated with a Communications degree from Pratt Insitute, focusing on Graphic Design. He's worked on magazines, non-profits, start-ups, agencies, and has most recently landed in tech.

Currently, David works full-time at Yodle (recently acquired by Web.com) supporting the marketing team with emails, landing pages, and other web collateral. He also helps the front-end team develop the internal Design System which they have unofficially branded "Designcraft."

When he isn't in front of his computer you can spot him at Brooklyn Boulders, slacklining/biking/jogging in Prospect Park, or just walking about with his dog Buster & beautiful fiancé Amelia.
