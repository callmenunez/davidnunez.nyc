Welcome

This style guide is a resource for designers, product managers, and developers, providing a common language around Web.com. Consider this your one-stop-shop for guidelines and assets. We want to make it easy to integrate Web.com to your collateral and marketing needs while respecting our brand and legal/licensing restrictions.

Why reinvent the wheel?

We always strive to stay consistent with our design, however we noticed inefficiencies when it came to handing off assets from our designers to our developers. The styleguide tries to address some of these issues, and we noticed it could help out our partners as well. When working on a new feature, the designer doesn’t need to think about what font-size, button styles, or colors to use. It’s all here! One less thing for them to worry about, phew.

How to Use This

As Web.com continues to grow, both as a product and a company, one challenge we are faced with is learning how to refine our brand identity and apply it cohesively to each of our products. We created this styleguide to act as a central location where we house a live inventory of branding, writing guidelines, templates and more. Anyone working with Web.com products is encouraged to stay familiar with this styleguide and help ensure that it is kept up-to-date.
